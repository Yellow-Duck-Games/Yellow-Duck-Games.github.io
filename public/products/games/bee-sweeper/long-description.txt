Welcome to Bee Sweeper, a cozy honeycomb twist on the classic minesweeper puzzle! Somewhere in the hive, sleepy bees are hiding — your job is to clear every cell of the honeycomb without waking a single one. Each number tells you how many bees are buzzing in the neighboring cells, so put on your thinking cap, read the clues, and sweep your way to victory. One wrong tap and… bzzzz! Luckily, a couple of spare hearts give you room for the occasional slip. Play across beautifully hand-crafted meadows and unlock delightful themes — from golden Beehive and blooming Flowers to fresh Spring and a sleek Dark mode. With soothing scenery, snappy one-touch controls, and puzzles that are easy to learn but satisfyingly tricky to master, Bee Sweeper is the perfect game to unwind with. Best of all — no ads, no pop-ups, no annoying stuff. Just you, the bees, and the honeycomb. Can you sweep them all?
